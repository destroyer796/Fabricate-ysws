# Rewards!

For every cool feature you ship your printer with, you'll get points which you can spend in the shop. These points can be used on tools to help assemble your printer, extra grant money to fund new features, or even an entire 3D printer to help you prototype parts. I'd love to see some super cool features like a custom toolchanger!

## Features:
- Unique motion system - 100 points
- Bed probe - 20 points
- Enclosure - 50 points
- Auto bed levelling - 30 points
- Toolchanger - 240 points
- Multi-color - 150 points
- Camera - 10 points
- Filament detection - 15 points
- Custom PCB - 50 points
- Custom Mainboard - 350 points

For your feature to be rewarded, it must be custom designed and polished. You can't just say you're going to mount a bed sensor with tape, you should design a mount and add it to your firmware. These must be your own designs.

If you design a feature you think is worthy of bonus points, ask and we'll add it to the list! We want to reward printers that go the extra mile, so any polished, cool feature is eligible.
